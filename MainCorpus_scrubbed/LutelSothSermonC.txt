blisse þat he hedde to ȝiuernesse and prude none neode he nedde he nom þen appel of þe tre þat him forbode was so reusful dede idon neuer non nas he made him into helle falle and efter him his children alle þer he was fort ure drihte hine bohte mid his mihte he hine alesede mid his blode þat he schedde upon þe rode to deþe he ȝef him for us alle þo we weren so stronge atfalle alle bacbiteres wendet to helle robberes and reueres and þe monquelle lechurs and horlinges þider sculen wende and þer heo sculen wunien euere buten ende alle þeos false chepmen þe feond heom wule habbe bachares and brueres for alle men heo gabbe loȝe heo holdet hore galun mid berme heo hine fulleþ and euer of þe purse þat seluer heo tulleþ boþe heo makeþ feble heore bred and heore ale habben heo þat seluer ne tellet heo neuer tale godemen for godes luue bileueþ suche sunne for atten ende hit binimeþ heueriche wunne alle prestes wifes ich wot heo beoþ forlore þes persones ich wene ne beoþ heo noȝt forbore ne þeos prude ȝungemen þat luuieþ malekin and þeos prude maidenes þat luuieþ ianekin at chirche and at cheping hwanne heo togadere come heo runeþ togaderes and spekeþ of derne luue hwenne heo to chirche comeþ to þe haliday eueruch wile his leof iseon þer ȝef he may heo biholdeþ wadekin mid swiþe gled eye atom his hire noster biloken in hire teye masses and matines ne kepeþ heo nouht robin wule gilot leden to þen ale and sitten þer togederes and tellen heore tale he mai quiten hire ale and soþen do þat gome an eue to go mid him ne þunchet hire no schome hire sire and hire dame þreteþ hire to bete nule heo forgo robin for al heore þrete euer heo wile hire schere ne com hire nomon neh fort þat hire wombe up arise an heh godemen for godes luue bileueþ eoure sunne for aten ende hit binimeþ heueriche wunne bidde we seinte for hire milde mode for þe teres þat heo wep for hire sone blode al so wis so he god his for hire erndinge to þe blisse of heuene he us alle bringe amen
